Music

All my life I have grown up with music being a huge influence on everything I do. For as long as I can remember, every saturday I would be woken up to go do my chores with a song in the kitchen playing. Playing, listening, and composing music captivates me for hours at a time. This page hopfully gives you a view into the important music of my life.

Here are some of the ways music has touched me:
  1. Listening
    • In middle school I was able to hear a song and quickly pick it out on the piano.
    • While still a todler I remember my mom taking me to music class. It is my earliest memory.
      • This shows just how important music is in a child's development and memory
      • Rythym became instilled in my heart early
  2. Learning
    • I started learning piano when I was 5 and continued to add more instruments and pieecs to my repertoire.
    • These are some of the classical pieces I've learned and performed:
      • Chopin - Fantaisie-Impromptu (Op. 66)
      • Debussy - Gradus Ad Parnassum
      • Rachmaninoff - Prelude, Op. 23, No.
      • Chopin - Nocturne in E minor, Op. posth. 72
      • Debussy - Clair de lune
  3. Playing
    • After learning the basics of piano and later guitar, composing became one of my favorite past times.
    • I composed originals and renditions of these and many more:
      • God Be With You Till We Meet Again
      • Nearer, My God to Thee
      • Come Follow Me
      • Sweet Hour of Prayer
      • Colors of the Wind
      • Carol of the Bells
